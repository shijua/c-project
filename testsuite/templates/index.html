Welcome to the testing tool!

To test your implementation, please make sure that:

  • The /testserver/solution directory exists.
  • The /testserver/solution directory contains your solution and your Makefile
  • The /testserver/test/test_cases directory exists.
  • The /testserver/test/expected_results directory exists.
  • Your assembler is implemented in the assemble executable file.
  • Your emulator is implemented in the emulate executable file.


On the navigation bar you can choose to:

  • Compile Solution: compiles your solution.
  • Assemble Tests: test your assembler on all or on a specific test case.
  • Emulate Tests: test your emulator on all or on a sepcific test case.
  • Instruction Manual: view the instruction manual.


When assembling/emulating, you will be prompted to another page that will display the results of your assembler/emulator. The results can be one of the following:

  • CORRECT: assembler/emulator produced the correct result.
  • INCORRECT: assembler/emulator produced an incorrect result.
  • FAILED: assembler/emulator failed to assemble/emulate.


Assembling/Emulating all the tests can take some time.


When assembling/emulating a specific test you will also be able to inspect the expected solution and the solution that your assembler/emulator provided.
